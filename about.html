Cook Cheap

About Cook Cheap

The original idea here was to create an application that would somehow make the lives of students easier. As a student myself, I found that I was constantly eating out, because I was running out of ideas of what to make. As a result, I was spending more money than I wanted to on food. To solve this problem, I created Cook Cheap, an application to help create a weekly meal plan. The idea is pretty simple, you can add filters based on a weekly budget and nutritional goals, and Cook Cheap will automatically create a customized plan with recipes and a weekly shopping cart to bring to the grocery story.

Future Plans

As of now, this page is just loading hard-coded sample data. In the future, I plan to connect with a recipe API to dynamically choose what recipes to show to a user. Additionally, I want to implement functionality for users to further customize their plan after having it load. For example, they could decide that they don't like a particular recipe and remove it. In this case, the program would then recommend a new recipe to replace it.
